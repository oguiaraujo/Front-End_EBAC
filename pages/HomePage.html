About me

My name is José Guilherme Silva de Araújo. I am a university student and an artist; I have been drawing since childhood and have always had an affinity for technology. Although I graduated as a Technical Chemist from the Federal Institute of Rio Grande do Norte (IFRN), I later enrolled at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte (UFRN), where I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Information Systems. In parallel, I am studying front-end development, where most of my projects are themed around the game Genshin Impact because I find it aesthetically beautiful.


I strive to be good at what I do and to achieve my goals. Thank you for your attention, and see you at the next level.
